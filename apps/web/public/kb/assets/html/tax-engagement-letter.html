Scope of Services

QiAlly will provide the following services as selected below. Items not explicitly listed are outside the scope of this engagement unless amended in writing.

  • Federal income tax return preparation (Form 1040/1120/1120‑S/1065, as applicable)
  • State and local returns (e.g., Indiana IT‑20/IT‑65; county returns)
  • Employment/Payroll filings (Forms 940/941/W‑2/W‑3)
  • Information returns (Forms 1099/1096)
  • Fixed asset & depreciation schedules (Form 4562)
  • Bookkeeping tie‑outs & trial balance mapping
  • Tax planning and estimated tax computations
  • IRS/state notices response (non‑representation)
  • Audit defense/Power of Attorney (Form 2848)
  • Entity classification & elections (e.g., 2553)
  • Advisory on sales/use tax & nexus
  • Other (describe):

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide complete and accurate information, including Forms W‑2, 1099, 1095, K‑1, and relevant books/records.
  • Disclose all income sources, digital asset activity, foreign accounts, and nexus‑creating activities.
  • Review returns carefully before filing and approve in writing (e‑signature is acceptable).
  • Retain copies of all source documents for at least 7 years.
  • Respond promptly to information requests and notices.

QiAlly Responsibilities

  • Perform services in accordance with professional standards and applicable law.
  • Maintain confidentiality and protect client data with reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards.
  • Advise on positions that are reasonably sustainable; disclose when penalties may apply.
  • File returns electronically upon receiving signed authorization and full payment of fees.
  • Notify you of material errors discovered during engagement and recommended corrections.
